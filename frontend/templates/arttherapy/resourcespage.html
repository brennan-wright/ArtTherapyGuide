Resources

Psychology Today

Many clients that are looking for a therpaist use Phycology Today's therapist finder to search for providers in their area. We highly reccomend setting up a profile with as much information about your practice as is relevant.

American Art Therapy Association

The American Art Therapy Association has many tools and resources such as an academic journal, tools for your career, and guides to gaining licencure. In addition they host a variety of events and trannings to enable you to connect with colleagues and keep up with new tools and techniques.

Art Therapy Credentials Board

They do much more than just credentials! Trainning, and other resources are avalible here as well.

Track Your Hours

An excelent tool for any aspiring MFT or ATR-BC that enables you to track your client contact and supervision hours for licencure in CA, NY, or TX.
