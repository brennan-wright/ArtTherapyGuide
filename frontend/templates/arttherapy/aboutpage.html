We Are Transparancy Advocates

We created the Art Therapy Guide because we were looking for tools to improve our practice of art therapy, but could not find what we were looking for. Most of the resources we were able to find were old, outdated, and oftentimes not art therapy specific. Even when looking at the licencing boards, we just were not finding the kinds of resources we were looking for.

Our goal is simple. Provide resources and materials to help practicing and aspiring therapists use art in a therapeutic manner.
